Favourite foods:

I love almost all kinds of food but some of my favourites are:

Pierogi

Pierogi are traditional Polish dumplings made of dough and filled with various ingredients like potatoes, cheese, meat, or fruit, then boiled or fried. They're a popular comfort food in Central and Eastern Europe.

Burrito

A burrito is a Mexican dish consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped around a filling, typically including ingredients like beans, rice, meat, cheese, and salsa. It's a popular handheld meal, often served hot.

Cottage Pie

Cottage Pie is a British dish made with minced meat (usually beef) cooked in gravy, topped with mashed potatoes, and baked until golden. It's a hearty comfort food, similar to Shepherd’s Pie but uses beef instead of lamb.

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish featuring vinegared rice combined with ingredients like raw fish, seafood, vegetables, or eggs. It’s often served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger, and comes in various forms like nigiri, maki, and sashimi.
